Multisyllabic words


Multisyllabic words:
Multisyllabic words are words that have more than one syllable.
An open syllable is a syllable that ends with a vowel sound.
The "consonant with i-e" pattern refers to a spelling pattern in English where a consonant is followed by the vowels "i" and "e" to represent the long "i" sound.

Word meaning:

  1. Pride:
    Meaning: A feeling of deep pleasure or satisfaction derived from one's achievements.
    Sentence: She felt immense pride in her son when he graduated at the top of his class.
  2. Bundle:
    Meaning:A collection of things, or a quantity of material, tied or wrapped up together.
    Sentence: The hiker carried a bundle of sticks to use for the campfire.
  3. Unfortunately:
    Meaning: Used to express regret about an unfortunate or unlucky circumstance.
    Sentence:Unfortunately, the concert was cancelled due to bad weather.
